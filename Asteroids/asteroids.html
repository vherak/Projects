Asteroids

Welcome to Asteroids!!! Here's how you play the game:

Control the ship using the arrow keys and use the space bar to shoot.

Beware! Bigger asteroids can split into smaller ones

Once you shoot all the asteroids, you win the game!

The score is displayed at the top left and you only have 3 lives

If you get hit by an asteroid, your lives will be deducted!

Have Fun !
